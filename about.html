Services

High school

We know that your priority – as with all parents- is for your sons and daughters to be fulfilled, well qualified, and, above all, happy. With that in mind, we recommend to you the time-honoured and deeply rooted experience that the Keen British School education brings. We will live and breathe our philosophy and ethos which is to develop every aspect of young people’s lives with our content, with outstanding teaching, delivered by the very best experienced and highly qualified instructors, sourced globally. Students are opportune to take part in programmes of education that can lead them to some of the highest-ranked universities around the world. The High school is organized into three key stages; Key Stage one (Years 7-9) where we offer an adapted version of the National Curriculum of England. Students in year 9 write the Cambridge checkpoint examination as well as junior WAEC. Key Stage two (Years 10-11) where students write the IGCSE exams with CIE (Cambridge International Examinations). Key Stage Three (Years 12 -13) where we offer the International Diploma Programme as well as WAEC, SSCE, NECO, GCE. Through the secondary years, we provide students with a broad and balanced curriculum and discourage students from dropping a learning area.
